About Me

Hello! I'm Vatsal Mishra, currently a second-year student at Manipal University Jaipur, pursuing a BTech Hons in IoT/IS. My passion for technology and design has led me to specialize in web development and UI/UX design.

Educational Background

I completed my 10th grade at Presidium School in Gurugram, where I developed a strong foundation in academics and extracurricular activities. For my 12th grade, I attended Indraprastha Public School in Rohini, Delhi, which further shaped my academic journey and prepared me for higher education.

Professional Interests and Skills

My primary areas of expertise include:

  • Web Development: I enjoy building responsive and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • UI/UX Design: Crafting intuitive and engaging user interfaces is a key focus of mine.
  • IoT: My current studies involve exploring the vast potential of Internet of Things technologies.
  • Coding: I love coding, and my main language is Java. I am also developing skills in Python.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I have a keen interest in artificial intelligence. I believe AI has the power to transform industries and improve our daily lives in numerous ways.

Hobbies and Interests

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I aspire to combine my skills in web development, UI/UX design, and IoT to create innovative solutions that address real-world problems. I am particularly excited about the potential of AI and look forward to integrating it into my future projects.
